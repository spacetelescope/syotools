Information about the spectral templates: 

flam      Flat in F_lambda
qso       z = 0 QSO composite 
s99       10 Myr Starburst
o5v       O5V Star
g2v       G2V Star
g191b2b   G191B2B (WD)
gd71      GD71 (WD)
gd153     GD153 (WD)
ctts      Classical T-Tauri Star
mdwarf    M1 Dwarf
orion     Orion Nebula
nodust    Starburst, No Dust
ebv6      Starburst, E(B-V) = 0.6
hi1hei1   Galaxy with f_esc, HI=1, HeI=1
hi0hei1   Galaxy with f_esc, HI=0.001, HeI=1

Most of these templates are drawn from the STScI
pysynphot package. To request addtional spectral 
templates, you can do the following: 

(1) visit the #etcs_and_tools channel on the HWO Community Slack Team, 
(2) file a GitHub issue here with your specific request: 
     https://github.com/spacetelescope/hwo-tools/issues
